1 Wallet top-ups & failed charges

ACBuy runs most purchases off a prepaid balance. Load funds before you chase limited drops so domestic freight is not stuck waiting on a declined card.

  • Match the processor ACBuy shows for your region—methods rotate.
  • Instant vs delayed settlement depends on the rail, not the acbuy spreadsheet.
  • Keep receipts until the wallet total matches what you paid.

2 Refunds back to card or balance

Canceled orders and seller-side refunds usually land back in your ACBuy wallet first; withdrawals to original payment channels follow platform rules.

  • Never DM merchants on acbuyai.com for refunds—use ACBuy tooling.
  • Cross-border refunds may take several business days.
  • Chargebacks bypass us entirely; talk to your bank only as a last resort.

3 How long items can sit in warehouse

Free storage is a perk, not infinite. After the complimentary window, daily fees or disposal policies can apply—read the banner inside your parcel card.

  • Set calendar reminders a few days before free storage ends.
  • Combine parcels only after each SKU passes QC.
  • Disposal timelines are strict; don’t treat warehouse like long-term storage.

Tip: rehearse bulky orders before you lock a carrier—volume surprises are expensive.

4 Tracking silence after export

Handoffs between origin hubs, airlines, and local posts often lack scans for days. That is common, not automatically “lost.”

  • Compare idle time with the carrier’s published SLA.
  • Customs can black-hole tracking until release.
  • If silence exceeds the SLA window, open a ticket with screenshots.

5 Estimated vs rehearsed weight

acbuy spreadsheet rows may list guess weights for budgeting. ACBuy’s rehearsal uses tape, scales, and volumetric divisors—trust that before you pay air freight.

  • Overpaid postage often returns as wallet credit.
  • Shoes with double boxing jump volumetric fast.
  • Remove dust bags only if you accept creasing risk.

6 Customs spot checks (normal vs scary)

Random inspections add time without implying seizure. Only panic if tracking explicitly says so or ACBuy requests paperwork.

  • Keep invoices handy for high-declaration countries.
  • Perfume/electronics may need alternate lines.
  • Research de minimis thresholds before you ship.

7 Warehouse photos vs acbuy spreadsheet hype

Rows in the acbuy spreadsheet are breadcrumbs. Warehouse lighting, 360° spins, and measurement tools inside ACBuy are the courtroom.

  • Zoom pixels, not vibes.
  • Request retakes while the item is still on-site.
  • After you approve outbound shipping, returns get harder.

8 Damage after last-mile delivery

Photograph the outer carton, labels, and defects before you unbox fully. Carriers time-box claims tightly.

  • File inside ACBuy with the evidence pack they request.
  • Insurance may cap payouts—read the fine print.
  • Our Discord can moral-support, not override case outcomes.

9 Route insurance: when it helps

Optional coverage can soften loss or impact damage, but rarely covers “I changed my mind.”

  • High resale value parcels justify the premium.
  • Customs seizures are usually excluded.
  • Submit claims immediately—late uploads get denied.

10 Category bans & batteries

Even if Taobao lists it, your forwarder or country may block it—think aerosols, knives, oversized lithium packs.

  • Cross-check ACBuy’s prohibited list before you pay international postage.
  • When unsure, ask support with the listing URL attached.

11 Verification holds on big top-ups

Large first deposits sometimes trigger fraud reviews—have ID ready and expect delays.

  • Use the same name across card + account.
  • Splitting payments across many tiny charges can backfire.
  • If a charge fails twice, switch rails before retrying endlessly.

12 Duties, VAT, and broker fees

You—not the acbuy spreadsheet—own import compliance. Carriers may advance duties and invoice on delivery.

  • Model worst-case landed cost before you buy.
  • VAT OSS rules differ post-2021 for EU buyers.
  • Keep customs emails in case you need proof of payment.

13 Combining, rehearsal, picking a line

Think of this as a logistics mini-game: consolidate SKUs, rehearse dimensions, then pick speed vs safety.

  • Never combine before every SKU passes QC.
  • Rehearsal fees beat surprise volumetric bills.
  • Triangle routes exist—ask which lines fake invoices forbid.

14 Coupons & referral quirks

Promos change monthly. Stackability, minimum spend, and region locks are enforced automatically inside ACBuy.

  • Farmed referrals can trigger bans—play fair.
  • Read expiry timestamps in local time.
  • If a code fails, screenshot and ticket—Discord cannot fix billing.

The acbuy spreadsheet gets you to the right listing; ACBuy handles contracts with sellers, warehouses, and carriers. acbuyai.com never touches your funds—bookmark both sites, but pay only where SSL + wallet live.
