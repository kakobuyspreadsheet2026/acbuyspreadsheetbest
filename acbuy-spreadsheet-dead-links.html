Link hygiene

When ACBuy Spreadsheet rows go stale (and what to do)

April 7, 2026 · 4 min read

Sellers rename listings, swap batches, or delete pages overnight. A curated ACBuy Spreadsheet can lag reality by hours—sometimes days if nobody flagged the row yet.

Symptoms of a dying link

  • Mobile Taobao loads a totally different SKU.
  • Price jumps without a note in the sheet.
  • Seller chat mentions “new link” in broken English.

Self-check before you pay

Open the URL in a clean browser session, confirm size charts, and screenshot the listing. If anything drifts from the row’s notes, pause and ping Discord—someone else may already be investigating.

How we mark rows

Moderators may tag a row “verify,” archive it, or drop a replacement link in News. None of that replaces your own double-check before ACBuy charges domestic freight.

We cannot restore a seller’s deleted page. We can only keep the sheet honest as fast as humans notice.
