QC lens

Reading QC in an ACBuy Spreadsheet without fooling yourself

April 7, 2026 · 5 min read

Rows in the ACBuy Spreadsheet often include seller or community photos. Treat them as screening clues, not a guarantee. The only photos that should green-light international freight are the ones ACBuy captures after the item hits their warehouse.

What ACBuy Spreadsheet QC can do

It helps you reject obvious mismatches early: wrong silhouette, fantasy colorways, or sellers with no track record. Think of it as triage so you do not waste domestic shipping on a listing that already looks suspect.

What only ACBuy can show

Warehouse lighting, measurement tools, and 360° spins exist because your order—not a marketing render—is on the table. That is when you verify stitching, SKU tags, and accessory completeness.

  • ACBuy Spreadsheet QC narrows the field.
  • Warehouse QC signs off on the physical unit you funded.
  • After you approve outbound shipping, reversing course is painful.

Micro-checklist before you click “ship”

  • Does the warehouse set match the size and colorway you ordered?
  • Are defects cosmetic-only or structural?
  • Do labels match the batch chatter you relied on in the sheet?
